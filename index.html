ABOUT ME

Hello. My name is Noah Velasco.
I code. I fly fpv.


As an undergraduate studying Computer Science at UT El Paso, I have been heavily involved in a great many things since the dawn of my university career. I have participated in many events ranging from things like the 2018 William Lowell Putnam Math Competition to 2021 HPC Winter Classic Invitational to Collegiate Esports (CR6 S7). Of these great many things I have been fortunate to be a part of, I have found my two favorite things - programming and FPV.

Alongside school, I'm currently pursuing my twin passions of programming and flying FPV, aiming to create work that inspires or contributes for the betterment of society.
