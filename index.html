ABOUT ME

Hello. My name is Noah Velasco.
I code. I fly fpv.

I have participated in many events ranging from things like the 2018 William Lowell Putnam Math Competition to 2021 HPC Winter Classic Invitational to Collegiate Esports (CR6 S7). Alongside school, I'm currently pursuing my twin passions of coding and flying FPV, aiming to create work that inspires or contributes for the betterment of society.
