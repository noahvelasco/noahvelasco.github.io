Privacy Policy

Survey Data

The purpose of surveys are to gauge how effective campus spaces are to individuals in accordance with their University/Community-College affiliation. All responses provided in the survey will be collected and analyzed for the purpose of improving our product. Your responses will be used in aggregate with those of other survey participants to identify trends and patterns, and to inform our decision-making process.

Individual responses will not be given to any third party whatsoever. All responses will remain confidential.

Proceeding to the survey implies that individuals understand and agree to these conditions.

Email Collection

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. Any email addresses that you provide to us will be used solely for the purpose of communicating with you about our services or products. We will not sell, rent, or disclose your email addresses to any third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, theft, and disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at admin@campusbase.io.
