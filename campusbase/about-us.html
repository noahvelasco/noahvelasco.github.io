About Us

Our mission:

Our mission is to break down the barriers to campus accessibility, and empower students with disabilities to navigate campus confidently and independently.


Our History:

After visiting several universities across the United States, it became apparent that campus navigation posed a significant challenge, despite the availability of traditional maps. This was especially true for campuses that were expanding and undergoing constant changes. In response, we are developing a modern approach to campus navigation that not only addresses this issue but also caters to the needs of individuals requiring ADA accommodations.
